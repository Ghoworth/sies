University of Strathclyde
Partner

The University of Strathclyde is an internationally leading research organization based in Glasgow UK. The University of Strathclyde has one of the largest and best-equipped engineering faculties in the UK. It is the largest engineering department in Scotland, with five subjects ranked in the top 10 in the UK (The Times and The Sunday Times Good University Guide 2023) Strathclyde has one of the largest research portfolios in the UK. The faculty's engineering research has global impact and is responsible for some of the UK's most significant practical research.
